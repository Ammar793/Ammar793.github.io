About Me



A big sheet of paper in my closet would tell you that I'm an Electrical Engineering (with a minor in Technological Entrepreneurship) graduate from McGill University in Montreal, Canada. During my time at McGill University however, I developed a passion for software development and honed this passion through various projects, competitions, freelancing opportunities and internships, where I got to to learn from some of the best in the field while working with the latest technology.

I like to live my life on the steep edge of the learning curve so I am always looking to acquire new skills while continuing to polish the older ones. My current areas of interest include Human-Computer Interaction, Web Development and Robotics.
