Brain Computer Interface

A 3D game that uses your brain waves as input

A Brain-Computer Interface built with the intention to aid users with learning disabilities to train and improve their brain health over time. The system uses the OpenBCI Ganglion board and EEG sensors to measure brain activity and classify users' mental state. This information is then fed into Unity3D where it used to drive a game that encourages concentrated thinking while discouraging distracted thinking.
